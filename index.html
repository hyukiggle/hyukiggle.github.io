Seung Hyuk Lee

Ph.D. Student  ·  School of Integrated Technology, Yonsei University

I am a Ph.D. student at LMNTS Lab, Yonsei University, advised by Prof. Songkuk Kim. My research focuses on mechanistic interpretability and fairness in text-to-image diffusion models, with a particular interest in understanding and mitigating demographic biases in the MMDiT architecture of Stable Diffusion 3.5.

My work spans empirical bias measurement, training-free debiasing interventions, and interpretability-driven analysis of generative vision models.
